Reference

Failure-mode abbreviations

Twelve recurring categories distilled from real production requests.

TK-RTemporal — Recent — Newly emerged or recently changed entities, events, products, and trends.
TK-CTemporal — Current — Up-to-date states such as rankings, live events, locations, or social conditions.
EIKEntity & IP Knowledge — Accurate identity and appearance of named people, characters, brands, products, or landmarks.
CSVConcept & Symbol Visualization — Precise concepts, flags, symbols, and diagrams requiring exact representation.
FHAFactual & Historical Accuracy — Correct facts, periods, artifacts, scientific details, and historical depictions.
CSCultural Specificity — Authentic culture-bound practices, aesthetics, artifacts, and conventions.
VURVisual / UI / UX — Specific interfaces, layouts, rendering conventions, spatial logic, and visual relationships.
DVZData Visualization — Accurate charts, graphs, timelines, diagrams, and infographics.
THGText / Typography — Correct, readable, and stylistically appropriate text rendered inside images.
CCSComplex Composite — Coherent multi-element scenes, spatial arrangements, and cross-concept compositions.
VUAVague / Abstract — Visualizing underspecified ideas, moods, metaphors, and abstract concepts.
IRRImplicit Reasoning — Requirements that must be inferred rather than stated directly, including contextual and relational constraints.
